Amer Abbas

Chief Executive Officer

Amer Abbas attained MSc Mechanical Engineering Degree from UMIST (University of Manchester, Institute of Science and Technology), United Kingdom in 1992 after getting Bachelor in Mechanical Engineering from UET Lahore, Pakistan. Started his professional Engineering career in December 1989 at Pakistan Engineering Company and began to provide his valuable contributions initially in maintaining and planning of spares for Electrical Arc Furnaces, Overhead cranes and Air Compressor Pumps and later also worked in the field of maintenance of Electrical and Mechanical Installations in major Government buildings.

Muhammad Aslam attained Bachelor of Technology in Refrigeration and Air- Conditioning after getting Diploma in Associate Engineering in RAC from GCT Lahore, Pakistan. Started his professional career in 1982 at Orient Engineer (Pvt) Ltd as an Execution engineer and completed various projects efficiently in different cities of Pakistan. Besides project execution, he is also acting as a technical advisor in the operation and maintenance of Heating Ventilation and Air Conditioning Equipment and his contributions lead Captive Aire up to high technical standards.
